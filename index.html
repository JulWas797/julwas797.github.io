About Myself

My journey in coding began at a young age with basic command-line scripts, Scratch and AppInventor. Later on I put my focus to Visual Basic .NET. It allowed me to learn key programming concepts

Soon, I took a break from programming to explore the world of Microsoft Windows betas and internals. My focus shifted to graphic design with Adobe Photoshop and UI/UX prototyping with Sketch/Figma.

The thing that got me into returning was purchasing an old Samsung smartwatch. I created basic apps and watch faces, two of which are still available on Galaxy Apps with over 3000 total downloads.

I then focused on VB.net, C#, and after another break, went full-on Java. What I've also done was taking coding classes (learning Python for Machine Learning in the process), and reading different books that taught me things such as C Language.

Currently, I continue to learn and strive to improve. I view code as an art form and aim to provide the best user experience possible. In my free time, I enjoy listening to music, DJing, music production, graphics editing, watching anime, and reading books.

My goals include further improving my abilities and enhancing my knowledge by focusing on portfolio projects and learning industry tools such as Kubernetes.
